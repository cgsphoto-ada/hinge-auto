Brand tokens: phosphor hot-magenta/pink `#FF4FD8`, bright core `#FF8AEA`,
dim ink `#FFA8E8`, background `#000000`. CRT phosphor aesthetic — completes
the family alongside TerraPlayer (phosphor green) and OpenEdu (phosphor blue).

## Image-gen prompt — `hingeauto-wordmark.png` (hero, wide, on black)

> Wide banner logotype reading "HINGEAUTO" in a single line. Blocky pixelated
> bitmap typeface — chunky, low-resolution pixel letterforms like the VT323 /
> classic CRT-terminal / DOS-BIOS font, monospaced, uppercase, slightly wide
> letter-spacing. The letters glow in phosphor hot-magenta/pink (#FF4FD8) with a
> brighter #FF8AEA core and a soft outer bloom/halo, like illuminated text on an
> old CRT monitor. Pure black (#000000) background. Subtle horizontal CRT
> scanlines across the whole image, very faint screen vignette in the corners,
> and a slight red/cyan chromatic-aberration fringe on the letter edges. Crisp,
> legible, centered, with generous padding around the word. Retro-futuristic
> terminal aesthetic, flat 2D (no 3D bevel, no perspective), no reflections, no
> extra text or icons. Aspect ratio 3:1 (wide). High resolution. Spell the
> single word exactly, letter for letter: H-I-N-G-E-A-U-T-O.

> A wide, ultra-minimal retro-CRT banner illustration on a pure black (#000000)
> background, showing a left-to-right pipeline of four glowing hot-magenta/pink
> (#FF4FD8, with a brighter #FF8AEA core) wireframe pixel-art icons connected by
> simple glowing arrows: (1) a smartphone outline whose screen shows a
> dating-profile card, (2) a vertical stack of overlapping screenshot frames,
> (3) a glowing AI brain made of connected neural nodes, (4) a heart icon. All
> four icons rendered as flat 2D phosphor-glow line art with soft bloom, evenly
> spaced in a single horizontal row, centered vertically, generous padding.
> Faint horizontal CRT scanlines, subtle corner vignette, slight chromatic
> aberration. Absolutely no text, no words, no letters, no numbers anywhere.
> Aspect ratio about 3:1 wide. Retro-futuristic terminal aesthetic.
